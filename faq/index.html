Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

We will greet your team at your arrival hub in our top-of-the-line fleet. Relax in supreme comfort as we drive you to and from our remote site. We can welcome your group from hubs such as the area’s largest airport, a smaller municipal airport for charter flights, or the region’s main train station.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Is there a complex challenge on your team’s horizon? Has your team or organization recently added new members? Maybe your team is top-notch but you’d like to uplevel trust, or reframe your team culture as something truly special. We can help.

What size teams can you accommodate?

Our simple, comfortable cabins offer 12 private rooms in total. Each has an ensuite bath. We value your team's individual privacy as the perfect complement to group exercises and outdoor activities. For larger teams, we can customize the same program over multiple weeks to accommodate all team members.

Is there really no wifi available?

We’re (sort of) kidding about that! While our research has shown that stepping away from daily online routines is foundational to this team building, we understand that work still needs to get done. We have a dedicated computer room for your use, though we know you won’t regret it if you remain unplugged.
